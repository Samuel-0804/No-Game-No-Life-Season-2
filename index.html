Aschente! The Highly Anticipated Second Season is in Production

TOKYO, JAPAN – The wait is finally over. Kadokawa and Studio Madhouse are incredibly proud to announce that the television anime production for No Game No Life Season 2 has officially been green-lit.

Following the massive global success of the first season and the theatrical film No Game No Life: Zero, Sora and Shiro (『 』) will return to the world of Disboard to continue their conquest of the Sixteen Races. The new season will adapt the highly anticipated volumes of Yuu Kamiya's best-selling light novel series, pushing the boundaries of fantasy, strategy, and mind-bending games.

Director Atsuko Ishizuka and the visionary animation team at Studio Madhouse are returning to bring Disboard's vibrant, kaleidoscopic world back to your screens.

"To all the fans who have waited so patiently around the world: thank you. Your endless support and passion for Sora, Shiro, and the world of Disboard made this possible. The games are about to get much bigger, and I cannot wait for you to see what the anime team is bringing to life. Let the games begin anew!"

— Yuu Kamiya, Original Creator

Information regarding broadcast dates, streaming platforms, and key visuals will be revealed in the coming months. Please follow our official social media channels to be the first to know.
